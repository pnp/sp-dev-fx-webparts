VENDOR SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Vendor Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2024 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

Acme Corporation, a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 123 Technology Drive, San Francisco, CA 94105 ("Client")

AND

TechServe Solutions Inc., a California corporation with its principal place of business at 456 Innovation Boulevard, San Jose, CA 95110 ("Vendor")

§1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

Vendor shall provide software development, maintenance, technical support, system integration, and documentation services to Client in a professional manner according to industry standards.

§2. TERM AND TERMINATION

This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for two (2) years. The Agreement automatically renews for successive one-year periods unless either party provides ninety (90) days written notice of non-renewal. Either party may terminate for material breach upon thirty (30) days written notice if the breach is not cured.

§3. COMPENSATION AND PAYMENT

Client shall pay Vendor monthly fees as specified in each Statement of Work. Invoices are due within thirty (30) days. Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month. Client shall reimburse reasonable pre-approved expenses.

§4. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

§4.1 Limitation of Liability
EXCEPT FOR BREACHES OF CONFIDENTIALITY OR INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY, TOTAL LIABILITY SHALL NOT EXCEED AMOUNTS PAID IN THE PRECEDING TWELVE MONTHS, CAPPED AT TWO MILLION DOLLARS ($2,000,000).

§4.2 Exclusion of Damages
NO LIABILITY FOR INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES, INCLUDING LOST PROFITS OR DATA.

§5. CONFIDENTIALITY

Confidential Information means all non-public information marked as confidential. Receiving party must: (a) hold in strict confidence; (b) not disclose to third parties; (c) use only for Agreement purposes. Obligations survive for five (5) years after disclosure.

§6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Work product created for Client is owned by Client upon full payment. Vendor retains pre-existing IP and grants Client a non-exclusive, royalty-free license for use with the services.

§7. GENERAL PROVISIONS

Governed by Delaware law. Disputes subject to arbitration in San Francisco. This Agreement supersedes all prior agreements and may be amended only in writing.
