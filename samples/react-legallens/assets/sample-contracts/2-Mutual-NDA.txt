MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement is made as of March 1, 2024, by and between:

GlobalTech Innovations LLC, with offices at 789 Innovation Park, Boston, MA 02101 ("Party A")
AND
DataStream Analytics Corp., with offices at 321 Data Center Way, New York, NY 10001 ("Party B")

§1. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" means technical, business, financial information disclosed by one Party to the other, including:
- Trade secrets, algorithms, software, source code
- Research, development, designs, specifications
- Financial data, customer lists, business plans
- Information marked "Confidential" or reasonably considered confidential

Exclusions: Information that is (a) publicly available; (b) rightfully received from third parties; (c) independently developed; (d) known prior to disclosure; or (e) required by law to be disclosed.

§2. OBLIGATIONS

Receiving Party shall:
- Hold Confidential Information in strict confidence
- Not disclose to third parties without consent
- Use only for the agreed Purpose
- Disclose only to employees/contractors with need-to-know who are bound by confidentiality
- Not copy or create derivatives except as necessary

§3. OWNERSHIP AND RETURN

All Confidential Information remains property of Disclosing Party. Upon request or termination, Receiving Party shall return or destroy all Confidential Information and certify destruction.

§4. TERM

This Agreement commences March 1, 2024 and continues for three (3) years. Confidentiality obligations survive for five (5) years from disclosure date.

§5. REMEDIES

Breach may cause irreparable harm. Disclosing Party entitled to equitable relief including injunction, in addition to other remedies.

§6. GENERAL PROVISIONS

Governed by New York law. Exclusive jurisdiction in New York County courts. This Agreement is the entire agreement and may be amended only in writing.
